Burger Trivia

When did burgers first appear in the united states?

The origin of burgers in the United States is a matter of debate among historians, but it is generally believed that the hamburger sandwich as we know it today originated in the late 19th or early 20th century. One popular theory is that the hamburger was first served in the 1880s at a small diner in New Haven, Connecticut called Louis' Lunch. Another theory suggests that the hamburger was first served at the 1904 World's Fair in St. Louis, Missouri.
